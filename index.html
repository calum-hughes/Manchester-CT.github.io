This is the webpage of the University of Manchester Category Theory group. We are a subgroup of the University of Manchester's Logic group. Our work includes research into a variety of topics in category theory and its relation to logic, including the following topics:

  • Categorical Logic
  • Low-dimensional higher category theory
  • ∞-category theory
  • Models of dependent type theory
  • Duality Theorems
  • Topos theory
  • Categorical model theory

We also are a member of YAMCATS, a category theory research network between the universities of Leeds, Manchester and Sheffield.
